### 1. **Disease Detection** (Already in your app!)
**Location:** `ImageUpload.js` component
**Endpoint:** `/analyze-crop`
**How it works:**
- User uploads crop image via 📷 button
- Backend analyzes with CV model
- Shows crop type, fertilizer, irrigation advice

### 2. **LLM Chatbot** (Already in your app!)
**Location:** `FarmerChatbot.js` component
**Endpoint:** `/chat`
**How it works:**
- User asks questions in chatbot
- Backend provides intelligent answers
- Supports 4 languages (en, hi, te, ta)

### 3. **Comprehensive Analysis** (Already in your app!)
**Location:** `AutomaticDashboard.js`
**Endpoint:** `/api/comprehensive-analysis`
**How it works:**
- Analyzes soil + weather data
- Provides crop recommendations
- Shows fertilizer needs, soil health
